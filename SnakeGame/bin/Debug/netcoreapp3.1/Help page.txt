HOW TO PLAY
1. Move up   : \u1329
2. Move down : j
3. Move left : jk
4. Move right: nj

GAME RULES
1. The snake has lives if it hits one of the obstacles then decrease the snake's lives by 1
2. Every time the snake eat the food, the score will increase by 10
3. Game over if the snake's lives are 0
